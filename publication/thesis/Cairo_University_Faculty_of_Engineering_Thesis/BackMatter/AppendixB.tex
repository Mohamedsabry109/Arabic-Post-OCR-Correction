This appendix reproduces the complete Arabic-language diagnostic text
generated by \texttt{LLMInsightsLoader.format\_for\_prompt()} from the
PATS-A01 training-split insights file
(\texttt{results/phase4d/insights/PATS-A01\_insights.json}).
This text is injected verbatim into the Phase~4D system prompt as the
\texttt{\{insights\_context\}} placeholder (see Appendix~A.6).

The text is generated automatically from the per-error-type fix rates and
introduction rates computed during Phase~4D analyse-train mode. The
generation thresholds are: weakness threshold \texttt{max\_fix\_rate\_weakness
= 0.4} (fix rate below 40\% qualifies as a weakness);
over-correction threshold \texttt{min\_intro\_rate = 0.05} (introduction
rate above 5\% qualifies as an over-correction warning);
minimum sample size \texttt{min\_sample\_size = 10} (error types with fewer
than 10 baseline instances are excluded).

%────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section*{B.1 \quad Generated Arabic Diagnostic Text}
%────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The following text was injected into the Phase~4D prompt for all 200
evaluation samples in the PATS-A01 Akhbar training split:

\begin{quote}\itshape
  \RL{ملاحظات بناءً على تحليل أداء النموذج في تصحيح نصوص مشابهة:}

  \bigskip
  \RL{مواطن الضعف (أخطاء متكررة لم تُصحَّح — انتبه بشكل خاص):}

  \RL{• حذف الأحرف: يُخفق في التصحيح 97\% من الحالات}

  \RL{• أحرف النقاط (ف/ق، ب/ت/ث، ...): يُخفق في التصحيح 93\% من الحالات}

  \RL{• استبدالات متنوعة أخرى: يُخفق في التصحيح 84\% من الحالات}

  \bigskip
  \RL{تحذير من الإفراط في التصحيح (لا تغيّر الكلمات الصحيحة):}

  \RL{• حذف الأحرف: يُدخل أخطاء جديدة في 212\% من الحالات}

  \RL{• استبدالات متنوعة أخرى: يُدخل أخطاء جديدة في 163\% من الحالات}
\end{quote}

%────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section*{B.2 \quad English Translation}
%────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

\begin{quote}
  Notes based on an analysis of the model's performance in correcting similar
  texts:

  \bigskip
  \textbf{Weaknesses} (recurring errors that were not corrected --- pay
  special attention):

  \begin{itemize}
    \item Character deletion: fails to correct in 97\% of cases.
    \item Dot-group characters (fa/qaf, ba/ta/tha, \ldots): fails to correct
          in 93\% of cases.
    \item Other miscellaneous substitutions: fails to correct in 84\% of cases.
  \end{itemize}

  \textbf{Over-correction warning} (do not change correct words):

  \begin{itemize}
    \item Character deletion: introduces new errors in 212\% of cases.
    \item Other miscellaneous substitutions: introduces new errors in 163\% of
          cases.
  \end{itemize}
\end{quote}

corrected. Introduction rate is the fraction of correct tokens of each type
that the LLM incorrectly modified.

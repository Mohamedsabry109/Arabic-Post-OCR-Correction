\section{Another Section Example}\label{sec_sec2label}
You can create sub-sections and sub-sub-sections as follows.
\subsection{Sub-Section 1 Example}\label{subsec_subsec1label}
\subsection{Sub-Section 2 Example}\label{subsec_subsec2label}
\subsubsection{Sub-Sub-Section 1 Example}\label{subsubsec_subsubsec1label}
\subsubsection{Sub-Sub-Section 2 Example}\label{subsubsec_subsubsec2label}
You can add a figure that takes the whole text width as follows. 
\begin{figure}
\centering
\includegraphics[width=\linewidth]{Figures/Ch1/fig1.jpg}
\caption{Text Width Figure Example}
\label{fig_fig1label}
\end{figure}
You can refer to the figure using its label as follows:

As shown in Fig. \ref{fig_fig1label}, .....

Also, you can add a figure that takes a specific width as a ratio of the whole text width as follows. 

\begin{figure}
\centering
\includegraphics[width=0.5\linewidth]{Figures/Ch1/fig2.jpg}
\caption{Half Text Width Figure Example}
\label{fig_fig2label}
\end{figure}

Also, you can add a figure with sub-figures as follows. 

\begin{figure}
\centering
    \begin{subfigure}{0.3\textwidth}
    \centering    
    \includegraphics[width=\linewidth]{Figures/Ch1/fig2.jpg}
    \caption{Sub-Figure 1 Caption}
    \label{subfig_subfig1label} 
    \end{subfigure}
\hspace{0.5in}%horizontal space between figures
    \begin{subfigure}{0.3\textwidth}
    \centering
    \includegraphics[width=\linewidth]{Figures/Ch1/fig2.jpg}
    \caption{Sub-Figure 2 Caption}
    \label{subfig_subfig2label}
    \end{subfigure}
\caption{Figure With two Sub-Figures Example}
\label{fig_fig3label}
\end{figure}

you can refer to the figure as a whole (Fig. \ref{fig_fig3label}) and you can refer to each sub figure (Fig. \ref{subfig_subfig1label} and Fig. \ref{subfig_subfig2label}).

You can make a rotated figure as follows:
\begin{sidewaysfigure}
\centering
\includegraphics[width=\textwidth]{Figures/Ch1/fig1.jpg}
\caption{Rotated Figure Example}
\label{fig_rotatedfiglabel}
\end{sidewaysfigure}
